Mechanics

There are 6 types of elements: fire, cold, water, lightning, dark, and light.
The more elements there are in the ecosystem, the more monsters are spawned in new rooms and the more degraded crafted weapon gets.

To craft stuff you need required items and required elements to be put into cauldron. You put elements into cauldron by putting items into it - items will be destroyed and cauldron will receive elements contained in destroyed items. When you leave the room all unused elements in cauldron are released into the ecosystem.

When you leave the room:
- all monsters healed to full and returned to their starting positions
- all the items destroyed and their elements added to ecosystem

Controls

WASD to move.
E - open/close inventory.
Q - open/close minimap.
C - open/close crafting menu.
Esc - close window in focus / pause and settings if no windows open
Right click to use item.
Space - slow time.
Space+(letter) - use of the item in the related inventory slot (inventory shaped like keyboard).
Alt - show info (hp bars, elemental saturation of items in the inventory).
Alt+(hover over item/monster) - show info about the item/monster (flags on the right show elemental saturation).

Timer for speedruns can be enabled in settings.

Tips and Tricks

Use boars and golems to easily murder other monsters in the room.
Don't forget to throw rocks into your enemies.
Light is a good element to throw into ecosystem - treants easy to kill and have good drop.
Hell is a good place to farm fire element for crafts.
Dark affects spawn rate 3 times stronger than other elements.
Check resistances - correctly crafted hammer can one-shot imps...
